Welcome to CRY IIT Roorkee
A group of motivated volunteers ensuring lasting change for children



We are a group of volunteers currently studying in IIT Roorkee working to enable peoples' collectives and movements encompassing diverse segments, to pledge their particular strengths, working in partnership to secure, protect and honour the rights of under privileged children in our society, especially around Roorkee.

WE CAN HELP

What we do

We conduct surveys to identify the children who need help, get children admitted to schools through RTE, help them in studies through mentorship and Work to create a more aware society in a whole.

Learn More

How we do

We get help from the students and the professors in the campus who come forward to support us in our initiatives. We also set up stalls during fests to raise awareness for this great cause.
